# Future Phases

This document captures features intentionally deferred from the production MVP.

## Deferred from MVP

- Student and guardian portal access.
- External student and guardian notifications.
- Student self-booking and lesson request approvals.
- Full invoice creation and payment tracking.
- Events, clinics as standalone event workflows, and horse shows.
- Chores, tasks, volunteer tracking, and partner organization access.
- File uploads and MinIO-backed document/photo storage.
- Structured horse history, including awards, competition records, veterinary history, and photo galleries.
- Historical lesson import before go-live.
- Google Calendar synchronization.
- Drag-and-drop calendar rescheduling.
- Whole-series recurring lesson editing.
- Package-level lesson credit allocation and automatic expiration enforcement.
- Structured arena/location scheduling with location conflict prevention.
- Structured horse lease management, including lease fees, lease date ranges, lessee records, self-practice time limits, and lease-specific scheduling constraints.
